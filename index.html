About Job Navigator

Job Navigator is a dedicated platform created specifically for OFPPT trainees to bridge the gap between education and professional employment. We understand the unique challenges faced by technical and vocational students in today's competitive job market.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of professionals by connecting them directly with industry partners who value the hands-on skills and practical knowledge that OFPPT graduates bring to the workplace.
